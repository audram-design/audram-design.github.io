Active Shooter Workflow Application

Android & Web



Value Proposition

Squire's Active Shooter Module delivers real-time situational awareness, enabling law enforcement teams to respond faster and more effectively during critical incidents. The module provides live updates on shooter locations, tracks all response units, and integrates essential command tools, resulting in more coordinated, informed decision-making.

By minimizing communication delays and optimizing resource allocation, Squire's solution enhances inter-agency coordination, preserves radio bandwidth, and ensures instant visibility of critical points, ultimately saving lives.





KPIs

  1. Response Time Reduction
    Decrease in incident response times by up to 40%, allowing teams to neutralize threats faster.

  2. Improved Inter-Agency Coordination
    30% increase in coordination efficiency among law enforcement, SWAT, EMS, and fire departments.

  3. Radio Traffic Reduction
    30% reduction in non-essential radio communication, freeing up channels for critical updates.

  4. Casualty Management Efficiency
    50% faster EMS response to casualty collection points, improving survival rates.

  5. Perimeter Security Accuracy
    100% real-time accuracy in establishing and adjusting secure perimeters for improved containment.
These measurable KPIs demonstrate the value Squire's Active Shooter Module brings to law enforcement, ensuring faster, more effective, and coordinated responses that save lives during high-risk situations.





MEDEVAC Workflow Application

Android & Web



Value Proposition

Squire Solutions' MEDEVAC Module transforms the speed and accuracy of battlefield medical evacuations by reducing response times from minutes to seconds. Our advanced digital platform provides law enforcement and emergency response teams with real-time communication tools, biometric tracking, and seamless system integration, allowing critical information to be transmitted and acted upon in under 1 minute.

This efficiency minimizes human error, reduces information gaps, and maximizes the golden hour for lifesaving medical intervention. With Squire Solutions, decision-makers receive up-to-the-second data, ensuring that medical teams are equipped with the information they need for rapid deployment and improved survival rates.





KPIs

  1. Time to MEDEVAC Launch
    Reduced from an average of 22.7 minutes to less than 1 minute.

  2. Error Reduction
    Virtually eliminates human error due to automated transmission of critical data.

  3. Golden Hour Utilization
    Maximizes available time for medical care by streamlining the entire MEDEVAC request process.

  4. Communication Efficiency
    Provides uninterrupted, real-time data sharing between teams in the field and command, reducing confusion and miscommunication.

  5. Biometric Tracking
    Continuously monitors patient vitals, allowing for faster and more accurate medical decision-making.
These KPIs track significant improvements in MEDEVAC operations, including a drastic reduction in time to launch from an average of 22.7 minutes to under 1 minute, the near elimination of human error through automated data transmission, maximized golden hour utilization by streamlining the MEDEVAC request process, enhanced communication efficiency with real-time data sharing between teams and command, and continuous biometric tracking for faster, more accurate medical decision-making.





UAS Workflow Application

Android



Value Proposition

Squire's UAS Module revolutionizes how operators interact with drones by shifting from a 1:1 control model to a 1:Many system.
The platform enables seamless, AI-enhanced, semi-autonomous drone behaviors, allowing operators to manage multiple drones simultaneously while maintaining full situational awareness. Operators can control, monitor, and modify drone behaviors on the fly, ensuring faster, more effective mission outcomes.

Squire's UAS Module minimizes human workload, optimizes operational efficiency, and enhances real-time decision-making, making it an essential tool in dynamic environments.





KPIs

  1. Response Time Efficiency
    Time reduction in managing drone swarms compared to traditional single-drone control.
    Target: Reduce time to deploy multiple drones by 80%.

  2. Operational Coverage
    Number of drones controlled per operator.
    Target: Expand operator capabilities to control 1:Many drones seamlessly.

  3. Error Reduction
    Incidence of human error in drone mission control and sensor management.
    Target: Decrease operational errors by 90% due to AI-enhanced autonomous behaviors.

  4. Situational Awareness
    Number of operators receiving critical updates from drones in real time.
    Target: Ensure 100% of operators maintain full situational awareness of all drones.

  5. Mission Success Rate
    Percentage of missions successfully completed with drone swarms.
    Target: Achieve a 95% success rate in mission execution using the UAS Module.

  6. Airspace Deconfliction
    Time taken to achieve seamless deconfliction and prioritization of drone swarms.
    Target: Reduce airspace conflicts by 90%.
These KPIs track the effectiveness of Squire's UAS Module in transforming drone operations, increasing efficiency, reducing errors, and maximizing mission success.
